inksquid (WebGPU, PNG-8 output)

Loads a JPEG/PNG, runs the quantizer, and renders the result alongside the original. Recommended palette sizes: 64 (compact), 128 (default), 256 (high quality). This demo defaults to scolorq for best perceived quality (~+2 dB PSNR / +0.05 SSIM over blue-noise on natural photos). Switch to blue-noise for the fastest path, or floyd-steinberg (CPU-only) for heavily textured photos.
